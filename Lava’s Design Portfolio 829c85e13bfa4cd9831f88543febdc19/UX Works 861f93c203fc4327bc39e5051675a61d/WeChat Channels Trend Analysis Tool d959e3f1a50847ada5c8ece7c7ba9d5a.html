Product Description:

This product is a web-based trend analysis system for WeChat Channel contents. Users are able to view top-ranked videos which were popularly propagated in WeChat Channels. All videos are discovered, analyzed and then ranked by the system automatically with algorithms powered by AI (i.e. artificial intelligence), in order to create a view for Content Creators to discover the most liked video and topics.

Besides that, our system also provides a more convenient UI for uploading videos to WeChat Channels, while users were only limited to so do on a mobile-based WeChat iOS/Android app before our launch. This alternative reduces the processing of exporting video contents from desktop and transferring, uploading via WeChat app, to just a few single clicks on a more effective web-based UI.

We mainly provide 4 functionalities on phase one product launch:

  • Overall Video Ranking
  • Video Hashtag Ranking
  • Content Creator Monitor
  • Upload Videos

Background

WeChat (the most popular instant messaging app in China) released Channels in Feb 2020 which provides a platform for users to share and browser short videos within the WeChat app, where we have seen similar functionality in TikTok, Instagram (IG Story), and Facebook.

Only 7 months after its launch, Channels reached 200M+ DAU, and over 6 million created videos. Content Creators on Channels, use this platform to sell their ideas, enhance company branding, or even makes commercial incomes.

Pain Points Analysis

We reveal two main pain points from users during our investigation before executing the product plan.

  • Users were constrained to upload video content within the WeChat app while they mostly edit those short videos on desktop, which costs a non-necessary file transferring process.
  • Unlike Douyin(抖音) or TikTok, WeChat Channels are a brand-new platform for short video sharing. Content Creators do not have an effective tool to keep a sight on the overall trends of content propagating, nor getting statistics data from their viewers.

User Persona

Our goal users are the Content Creators who wants to learn more about their viewers and the global trends in WeChat Channels.

Website Architecture

The whole website is mainly 4 sections:

  • Overall Ranking Videos
  • Top Ranking Tags
  • Monitor Creators
  • Upload Videos

Meanwhile, the Video Details and Creator Details play important roles in the product Where the users can observe the valuable data of the specific videos and the creators.

Sketching

1. 综合排行榜 Overall Trend： Displaying the top 200 overall ranking videos.

2. 标签排行榜 Tags Trend: Display the top 20 ranking tags and the top ranking videos under each tag.

3. 博主监控 Creator Monitor: Set the monitor schema for each Creator. Once the monitoring creators published the videos, the user can receive the alerts and notice.

4. 上传视频 Upload Video: User can upload the videos directly from the PC device.
